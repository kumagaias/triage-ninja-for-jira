## Recording Tips

1. **Screen Resolution**: 1920x1080 (Full HD)
2. **Frame Rate**: 30 FPS minimum
3. **Audio**: Clear narration with background music (low volume)
4. **Cursor**: Use cursor highlighting for important clicks
5. **Transitions**: Smooth transitions between scenes
6. **Text**: Use large, readable fonts
7. **Branding**: Consistent color scheme (Ninja theme)

## Equipment Needed

- Screen recording software (OBS Studio, Camtasia, or ScreenFlow)
- Microphone for narration
- Video editing software (DaVinci Resolve, Adobe Premiere, or iMovie)
- Background music (royalty-free)

## Post-Production

1. Edit video to 3-5 minutes
2. Add background music
3. Add on-screen text and annotations
4. Add transitions and effects
5. Export in 1080p MP4 format
6. Upload to YouTube/Vimeo
7. Add to README.md and Devpost submission
